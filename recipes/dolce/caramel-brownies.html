Caramel Brownies

Surrender to your chocolate cravings and make these fudgy, caramel brownies topped with flaky sea salt. You'll be glad you did! You might be tempted to keep such a decadent dessert all to yourself, but caramel brownies are a quintessential crowd-pleaser! Make a few batches to share as Valentine's gifts for the loved ones in your life, package them for a bake sale or serve them up at a birthday party. Who says cake gets to have all the fun?

Ingredients

  • 1/3 cup caramel sauce, plus more for drizzling
  • 1 1/4 cup (168g) all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 cup (24g) Dutch processed cocoa powder
  • 3/4 cup (168g) unsalted butter
  • 3 ounces unsweetened chocolate, chopped
  • 1 1/4 cups (260g) granulated sugar
  • 1/4 cup (46g) brown sugar
  • 1 teaspoon kosher salt
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 3 large eggs
  • 1/2 to 3/4 teaspoon flake salt, for topping (optional)

Directions

  1. Step 1

    Make homemade caramel sauce and let it cool.

  2. Step 2

    The batter will be thick, that's how you want it.

  3. Step 3

    Dot the top of the batter with caramel.

  4. Step 4

    Swirl the brownie and caramel together.

  5. Step 5

    Let the brownies cool to room temperature before slicing.

  6. Step 6

    Once baked, drizzle with extra caramel and sprinkle with sea salt.
